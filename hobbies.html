My Hobbies

I’m also a big fan of combat sports and regularly watch UFC fights and professional boxing matches. In addition, I like swimming, as it’s both relaxing and a great full-body workout.

  • Entertainment and Shows – In my free time, I enjoy watching anime and some of my favorite TV series. I particularly like shows such as Breaking Bad, Better Call Saul, and Two and a Half Men. These shows have great storytelling, interesting characters, and a mix of drama, strategy, and humor that I really enjoy.

  • Love for Mountains and Nature – I have a strong love for nature, especially mountains and hills. I enjoy visiting peaceful, scenic places where I can explore the outdoors, enjoy fresh air, and take a break from the fast pace of city life. Being in the mountains helps me relax and feel refreshed.
